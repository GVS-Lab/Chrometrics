nmco.utils.Run_nuclear_feature_extraction.run_nuclear_chromatin_feat_ext(raw_image_path, labelled_image_path, output_dir)[source]¶

Function that reads in the raw and segmented/labelled images for a field of view and computes nuclear features. nmco.utils.summarising_features module¶

nmco.utils.summarising_features.summarise_feature_table(data)[source]¶

Function that summarises distribution characteristics for all columns in a feature table. Measures computed are median, min, max, standard deviation (SD) Coefficient of Variation (CV) and Coefficient of Dispersion (CD), Inter_Quartile_Range(IQR) and Quartile Coeeffient of Dispersrion (QCD).

Args:

data: feature table with the columns of interest plust label column for nuclear idenity
